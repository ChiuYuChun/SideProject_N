Important Propaganda Common Fraud Discourses

99% of the time you see or hear the following keywords, it's a scam

  • 1. Stable profit, no risk
  • 2. Win the lottery for no reason, send you bitcoin, provide information to receive rewards
  • 3. Wealth freedom, sleep can benefit
  • 4. Registering an account can receive high remuneration
  • 5. Asking you to register a XEBIT account for no reason
  • 6. Find assistance from users with XEBIT accounts in social software
  • 7. Leasing an account as a trader can get a monthly commission
  • 8. If the account is frozen, you need to pay a deposit to unlock it

When you encounter the following action guidelines, please calmly stop, watch and listen, this is likely to be a scam

  • 1. Handsome guy with unknown origin, beautiful girl private message you for no reason
  • 2. Ask you to use the supermarket to pay
  • 3. Complete the payment within a limited time, with additional rewards
  • 4. Please provide your user account, which can help you make profits
  • 5. Unknown hyperlinks
  • 6. Scan the QR Code to receive the airdrop
  • 7. Asking you to provide your ID number, bank account
  • 8. On websites other than the official website of XEBIT, you are required to enter a password
  • 9. Fake the name of XEBIT official personnel, please provide your personal information or password
